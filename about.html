About Me

I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio and live with my husband, our yellow lab Bella and our tiny cat Olive. I've lived in Colorado and South Florida and been fortunate to have done some traveling around the world, spending time in Italy, Ireland, and Israel to name a few.

In my free time, I enjoy reading murder mysteries, working outside, developing my coding skills, eating great food and running. I am an avid runner and have run 2 marathons and a handful of half marathons to date.

I received my Master's degree in Speech Language Pathology and have worked with both children and adults for over 15 years in a variety of settings. My current position is Lead Speech Language Pathologist at a rehabiliation and long term care center for adults. I have enjoyed providing direct patient care to help patients improve their lives, educating families, working on an interdisciplinay team, and leading others over the years but am now ready to make a significant transition with my career.

I am grateful that I accidently found the world of coding and have immensely enjoyed learing and developing my coding skills over the last 4 months through SheCodes Workshops. I am amazed at how much I have learned in a such a short time and how excited I am to continue to keep learning and refining my skills as I transition into a career as a Front-end Developer.
