Rishona Lee

Aspiring Front-end Developer based in the United States

My journey into the world of coding all started during a holiday weekend. As I was relaxing and scrolling through my phone, I came upon an article about a Speech Language Pathologist (my current area of expertise) who had switched careers and had become a Software Developer. After reading this article, I felt mystified about such a transition in careers, but also very intrigued. That night I began to investigate coding further and within a week, I had started an online workshop, with the goal to become a Front-end Developer. A wise man once told me when you are looking for answers you have to be open and be ready to listen to the Universe. I have no doubt that the Universe pointed me in this unexpected but very exciting direction. I have truly found a passion for coding and am excited to continue my learning journey.
