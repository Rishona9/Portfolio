Hi, my name is

Rishona Lee

I am a highly motivated, aspiring Front end developer based in the United States.

This journey into coding started during a holiday weekend. As I was relaxing and scrolling through my phone, I came upon an article about a Speech Language Pathologist (my current area of expertise) who had switched careers and became a Software Developer. After reading this article, I thought to myself, "what a weird transition." Weird but also very intriguing. That night I began to research coding further and within a week, I had started an online Bootcamp, with the goal to become a front end developer. A wise man once told me when you are looking for answers you have to be open and listen to what the universe is telling you. I have no doubt that the universe pointed me in this unexpected but very exciting direction. I have truly found a passion for coding and am excited to continue my learning journey. Please enjoy my portfolio!
